Factory Farm Impact Calculator

Industrial animal agriculture is one of the leading contributors to climate change, responsible for at least 11 percent of global greenhouse gas emissions (World Animal Protection). That’s nearly as much as the entire global transportation sector.
We often worry about the climate footprint of the planet’s 7.8 billion people but overlook the environmental consequences of sustaining more than 70 billion farm land animals each year (New Roots Institute).
